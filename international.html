Current requirements for DGCA CPL licences:
Total Time 220 hrs, which includes the following:

1. Pilot in command: 120 hrs

2. Simulator: 20 hrs

3. Dual training Multi Engine: 10 hrs

4. Dual training Single Engine: 70 hrs

5. Relevant paperwork and certificates will be provided to ease the conversion process once the course is completed
